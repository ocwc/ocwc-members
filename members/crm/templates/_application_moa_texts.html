We are pleased to cooperate with universities and organizations around the world as members of the OpenCourseWare Consortium. As part of this cooperation, we agree to the following:

  1. We will demonstrate significant commitment to the production, use and promotion of OpenCourseWare. Such demonstration could include the development and maintenance of an OpenCourseWare site under our institutional name that is consistent with the definition of OpenCourseWare on the Consortium Web site, significant incorporation of OpenCourseWare materials into institutional offerings, active promotion of OpenCourseWare use or production, or other evidence as determined by the Consortium's Board of Directors and posted on the Consortium's website.
  2. We will work with other members to develop best practices to promote the development and use of OpenCourseWare. Opportunities to participate in working groups, committees and communities of interest are organized by the Consortium to facilitate these efforts.
  3. We will pay annual membership dues as set by the Consortium's Board of Directors.

As long as we adhere to the above criteria, we understand that the OpenCourseWare Consortium will allow us to display the OpenCourseWare Consortium logo on our OCW website. The logo will link directly to the OpenCourseWare Consortium Web site. Additionally, the OpenCourseWare Consortium will feature our institutional logo and profile in the members list on the website, which will link to our institutional OpenCourseWare site or other institutional web page. We understand that the OpenCourseWare Consortium can require us to remove the OpenCourseWare Consortium logo from any and all of our sites, and will remove our name and logo from its website, should we no longer meet the criteria above.

The applicable membership fee will be due upon the acceptance of our institution as a member of the OpenCourseWare Consortium.

The schedule of membership fees per calendar year can be found on the Consortium's website, and is subject to revision by the Consortium's Board of Directors. Membership fees for the current year are:

We are pleased to cooperate with other leading organizations in substantial economic support of the international OpenCourseWare Consortium. As part of this cooperation, we agree to the following:

  1. We will work with members of the OCW Consortium (and of other, related organizations) to develop best practices to promote reasonable consistency in quality, content depth, vocabulary, and presentation, all in the interest of offering users a similar experience from one member's OpenCourseWare site to another.
  2. We understand that the access to OCWC members granted to us through this membership must conform to the conditions set forth in the annually renewed Corporate Benefits Policy.
  3. We understand that in accepting this membership category, we waive all voting rights in the OCW Consortium. We will not attempt to use our economic support as the means to unduly influence any decisions of the OCWC Board of Directors.

So long as we adhere to criteria 1 and 2 above, we understand that the OpenCourseWare Consortium will allow us to display the OpenCourseWare Consortium logo on our website. The logo will link directly to the OpenCourseWare Consortium Web site. We understand that the OpenCourseWare Consortium can require us to remove the OpenCourseWare Consortium logo from any and all of our sites should we no longer meet criteria 1 and 2 above.

Please note that Corporate Members do not have voting privileges within the organization.
