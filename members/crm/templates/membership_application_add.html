Institutional Members are accredited post-secondary institutions that demonstrate significant support for the production, use and/or promotion of Open Education.

Associate Consortium members are groups of Institutional or Organizational members who have formed into an Association based on common interests.

Associate Consortia must have a minimum of 5 Institutional or Organizational members affiliated with them to remain in good standing.

Organizational members are institutions or organizations that demonstrate significant support for the production, use and/or promotion of Open Education. Accredited post-secondary institutions may join as Institutional members.

Corporate members provide services or materials that further the mission of the OCW Consortium and direct support of the Consortium in the form of higher annual dues.
